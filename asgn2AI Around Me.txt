2. AI Around Me (10 Examples)
Google Search – Uses ML ranking algorithms to show most relevant results.
YouTube Recommendations – ML predicts videos based on watch history.
Google Maps – Uses ML to predict traffic and fastest routes.
Instagram Feed – ML ranks posts based on user interaction.
Voice Assistants (Alexa/Google Assistant) – Uses NLP to understand speech.
Spam Email Filter – ML classifies emails as spam or not.
Netflix Recommendations – Suggests movies based on user behavior.
Online Shopping (Amazon/Flipkart) – Recommends products using ML models.
Face Unlock in Phones – Uses computer vision and ML for recognition.
Autocorrect/Keyboard Suggestions – Predicts words using language models.
